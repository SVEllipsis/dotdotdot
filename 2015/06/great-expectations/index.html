Great Expectations

Posted on June 04, 2015

After almost a week of work and waiting on parts in the Millbrook boatyard, SV Ellipsis is no longer a boat of the future - the electric generator has been ripped out and propulssion now in the hands of something much more ususal in the world of marine engineering - a pair of Tohatsu outboard motors. Thursday evening we sprung from Millbrook and motored the two miles to Mayflower Marina on the other side of the Plymouth Sound.
